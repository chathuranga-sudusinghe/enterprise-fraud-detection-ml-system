# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, provide constructive and specific feedback, and remain focused on improving the project.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, spam, and unrelated promotion are not acceptable in issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove inappropriate content and may close issues or pull requests that violate this code of conduct or fall outside the project scope. Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project participation.

Concerns should be reported privately to the maintainer through an appropriate contact method listed on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as practical.
